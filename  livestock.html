Livestock Farming

Cattles

Adding value and milk level production

instructions

step 1

    1.Select breed,feeding

Selecting a breed: Choose a breed of cattle that is suited to the local environment, intended purpose, and management practices. Providing adequate housing and facilities: Provide suitable housing and facilities for the cattle, including shelter, feeding and watering equipment, and a clean and safe environment. Feeding: Provide the cattle with a balanced and nutritious diet, including grass or hay, and supplementary feed such as grains, minerals, and vitamins as required.

step 2

    2.Health maanagement and Parasite control

Develop a regular health management plan, including vaccinations, deworming, and other preventive measures to protect against diseases and parasites.

step 3

    3.Marketing

Identify potential markets for the sale of cattle or their by-products, and develop a marketing plan that maximizes profits.

Step 4

    4.Financial Management

Financial management: Manage the financial aspects of the operation, including budgeting, accounting, and record-keeping, to ensure profitability and sustainability
